monday Ojonumi Oguche

Software Engineer student

Monday Oguche is a dynamic professional with a diverse background in pension consulting and a burgeoning interest in software engineering. Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, Monday developed a strong work ethic and a passion for problem-solving from an early age.

After obtaining a Bachelor's degree in building from federal university of technology minna niger state , Monday embarked on a career in pension consultancy, where he honed his analytical skills and gained invaluable experience in financial management and retirement planning. Over the years, he has helped numerous clients navigate the complexities of pension schemes and secure their financial futures.

Driven by a desire to explore new horizons and embrace emerging technologies, Monday is currently pursuing his interest in software engineering. With a keen interest in coding and a natural curiosity for technology, he is eager to leverage his analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities to excel in this dynamic field.

In his spare time, Monday enjoys participating in attending tech meetups, and immersing himself in online learning platforms to expand his knowledge of software development. He is excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the tech industry and is committed to continuous learning and growth.

As Monday embarks on this new chapter in his career, he remains dedicated to applying his expertise in pension consulting while embracing the challenges and opportunities of the ever-evolving world of software engineering.
